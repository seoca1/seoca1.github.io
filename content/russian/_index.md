## About Russian for English Speakers

Russian (русский язык) uses the Cyrillic alphabet — visually distinct from Latin script but learnable through transliteration. From a learner's perspective, the language has:

- **33 Cyrillic letters** (21 consonants + 10 vowels + 2 signs)
- **6 grammatical cases** (nouns change form by role: subject, object, possessor, etc.)
- **3 genders** (masculine / feminine / neuter)
- **Aspect-based verbs** (perfective vs. imperfective for completed vs. ongoing actions)
