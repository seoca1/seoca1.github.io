# Spanish Master Vocabulary List (Vocabulario Español)



## How to Use This List

1. **By topic:** Browse categories matching your current lesson
2. **By review cycle:** Pick one bundle from the rotation pool per day
3. **By frequency:** Categories 1–6 cover 80% of daily conversation
4. **By progression:** Move from category 1 (verbs) to category 11 (culture) over months

---

## 1. Daily Verbs (Verbos Cotidianos)

### Reflexive verbs (used for routines)

| Spanish | 1st person | English | Example |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **levantarse** | me levanto | to get up | *Me levanto a las siete.* |
| **ducharse** | me ducho | to shower | *Me ducho por la mañana.* |
| **vestirse** | me visto | to get dressed | *Me visto rápidamente.* |
| **acostarse** | me acuesto | to go to bed | *Me acuesto a las once.* |
| **irse** | me voy | to leave | *Me voy al trabajo.* |

### Meal verbs

| Spanish | 1st person | English | Example |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **desayunar** | desayuno | to have breakfast | *Desayuno café.* |
| **almorzar** | almuerzo | to have lunch | *Almuerzo a la una.* |
| **cenar** | ceno | to have dinner | *Ceno con amigos.* |

### General activity verbs

| Spanish | 1st person | English | Example |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **trabajar** | trabajo | to work | *Trabajo hasta las cinco.* |
| **estudiar** | estudio | to study | *Estudio español.* |

### Restaurant verbs

| Spanish | 1st person | Polite | English | Example |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **pedir** | pido | — | to order | *Pido la paella.* |
| **querer** | quiero | quisiera | to want | *Quisiera un café.* |
| **probar** | pruebo | — | to taste | *¿Puedo probar el vino?* |
