## About Japanese for English Speakers

- **3 writing systems:** Hiragana (46) + Katakana (46) + Kanji (2,000+常用)
- **SOV word order:** Subject-Object-Verb
- **Politeness levels:** です/ます (formal) vs. だ (casual)
- **Particles:** は・が・を・に・で・へ・と etc.
