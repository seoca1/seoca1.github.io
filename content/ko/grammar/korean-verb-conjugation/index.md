> 🇰🇷 **운영자용 한국어 요약**
> 1. 한국어 동사는 사전형이 **~다** 로 끝남 (e.g. 가다, 먹다, 공부하다)
> 2. 현재: ~아/어요 (가요, 먹어요, 공부해요) — 발음 규칙에 따라 합용
> 3. 과거: ~았/었어요 (갔어요, 먹었어요, 공부했어요)
> 4. 미래: ~ㄹ 거예요 OR ~겠어요 (갈 거예요, 공부할 거예요)
> 5. 높임말: ~습니다 (formal), ~아/어요 (polite), ~어 (casual)

# Korean Verb Conjugation

## 1. Dictionary Form (사전형)

Every Korean verb in dictionary form ends in **~다**:

| Dictionary | Meaning | Root |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **가다** | to go | 가 |
| **먹다** | to eat | 먹 |
| **공부하다** | to study | 공부하 |
| **좋아하다** | to like | 좋아하 |
| **예쁘다** | to be pretty | 예쁘 |

The **root** is what you conjugate. To find it, drop **다**.

## 2. Present Tense (현재 시제)

Add **~아/어요** to the root. Which vowel depends on the root's last vowel:

| Root vowel | Ending | Example |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **ㅏ, ㅗ** (bright) | ~**아**요 | 가**아요** → 가요 (go) |
| **other vowels** (dark) | ~**어**요 | 먹**어요** (eat), 마시**어요** → 마셔요 (drink) |
| **하다 verbs** | special: 해요 | 공부하**아요** → 공부해요 |

### Examples

| Dictionary | Conjugation | English |
|:---|:---|:---|
| 가다 | **가요** | I go |
| 먹다 | **먹어요** | I eat |
| 공부하다 | **공부해요** | I study |
| 마시다 | **마셔요** | I drink |
| 주다 | **줘요** | I give |
| 좋아하다 | **좋아해요** | I like |
| 운동하다 | **운동해요** | I exercise |

### Vowel Contraction (모음 축약)

When two vowels meet, they often contract:

| Combination | Result | Example |
|:---|:---|:---|
| ㅏ + 아 | 아 | 가 + 아요 → 가요 |
| ㅓ + 어 | ㅓ | 먹 + 어요 → 먹어요 |
| ㅣ + 어 | 여 | 마시 + 어요 → 마셔요 |
| ㅜ + 어 | 워 | 주 + 어요 → 줘요 |

## 3. Past Tense (과거 시제)

Add **~았/었어요** to the root:

| Root vowel | Ending | Example |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **ㅏ, ㅗ** | ~**았**어요 | 가**았어요** → **갔어요** (went) |
| other | ~**었**어요 | 먹**었어요** (ate) |
| 하다 verbs | 했어요 | 공부했어요 (studied) |

### Examples

| Dictionary | Past | English |
|:---|:---|:---|
| 가다 | **갔어요** | I went |
| 먹다 | **먹었어요** | I ate |
| 공부하다 | **공부했어요** | I studied |
| 마시다 | **마셨어요** | I drank |
| 주다 | **줬어요** | I gave |

## 4. Future Tense (미래 시제)

Korean future is **two patterns**:

| Pattern | Use | Example |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **~ㄹ/을 거예요** | Plan / prediction | **갈 거예요** = I'll go (decided) |
| **~겠어요** | Willingness / guess | **가겠어요** = I'll go (volitional) |

### ㄹ vs 을
- After vowel or ㄹ: **ㄹ** (가**ㄹ** 거예요)
- After other consonants: **을** (먹**을** 거예요)

| Dictionary | Future (Plan) | English |
|:---|:---|:---|
| 가다 | **갈 거예요** | I'll go |
| 먹다 | **먹을 거예요** | I'll eat |
| 공부하다 | **공부할 거예요** | I'll study |
| 자다 | **잘 거예요** | I'll sleep |

## 5. Negation (부정)

| Tense | Pattern | Example |
|:---|:---|:---|
| Present | **안 + verb** | **안 가요** (I don't go) |
| Present (formal) | **~지 않아요** | **가지 않아요** (I don't go) |
| Past | **안 + past** | **안 갔어요** (I didn't go) |
| Future | **안 + future** | **안 갈 거예요** (I won't go) |
| Can't | **~못 + verb** | **못 가요** (I can't go) |

> **안** = don't/won't (subjective — I choose not to)
> **못** = can't (objective — I'm unable to)

## 6. Honorifics (높임말)

Korean conjugates **for social status**. The most common levels:

| Level | Ending | Use | Example |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| **Formal (하십시오체)** | ~습니다 | News, speeches, very senior | **가습니다** |
| **Polite (해요체)** | ~아/어요 | Default everyday | **가요** |
| **Casual (해체)** | ~아/어 | Friends, younger | **가** |
| **Plain (반말)** | ~다 | Writing, internal | **간다** |

> English speakers must always default to **~요** form with strangers — using plain form with elders is **rude**.

## 7. Common Irregular Verbs

| Dictionary | Conjugation | Note |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **예쁘다** (pretty) | 예뻐요, 예뻤어요 | ㅡ → ㅓ/ㅐ drop |
| **고프다** (hungry) | 고파요, 고팠어요 | ㅡ → ㅏ |
| **아프다** (sick) | 아파요, 아팠어요 | ㅡ → ㅏ |
| **나쁘다** (bad) | 나빠요, 나빴어요 | ㅡ → ㅏ |
| **이쁘다** (cute) | 이뻐요 | same as 예쁘다 |

> The **ㅡ 불규칙 (eu-irregular)** drops ㅡ and adds ㅏ/ㅓ depending on the original vowel. Memorize as a group.

## 8. Practical Tips

- ☑️ **Always drop ~다 first** to find the root
- ☑️ **Check the last vowel** of the root — it determines 아/어요
- ☑️ **했어요 and 했었다** sound different — practice!
- ☑️ **Conjugate in patterns** — present → past → future → negative
- ☑️ **Stick with ~요 endings** until you're very confident
