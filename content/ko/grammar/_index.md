# Korean Grammar

Korean (한국어) grammar concepts for English-speaking learners. Korean grammar differs from English in three notable ways:

- **SOV word order** (Subject-Object-Verb) — the verb comes last
- **Particles** — case markers (은/는, 이/가, 을/를) follow nouns
- **Verb conjugation** — verbs change form based on politeness level
