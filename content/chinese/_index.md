## About Chinese for English Speakers

- **1 writing system:** Hanzi (汉字) — logographic characters (3,000+常用)
- **Romanization:** Pinyin (拼音) — Latin alphabet with tone marks
- **4 tones + 1 neutral:** Tone 1 (high flat) · Tone 2 (rising) · Tone 3 (dipping) · Tone 4 (falling)
- **SVO word order:** Subject-Verb-Object (similar to English)
- **Measure words:** 个/本/张/只/杯 etc. — required between numerals and nouns
- **No conjugation:** Verbs do not change form for tense/person/number
